What You'll Do

Thousands of uninhabited islands dot the emerald waters of Halong Bay. Cloaked in thick green vegetation, these limestone pillars have enchanted visitors for centuries. Experience this lush region with Getaway Halong Sapa, who have crafted a cruise that sails above all others - a journey that begins and ends in bustling Hanoi and devotes the rest of the trip to an exploration of Halong Bay aboard a luxurious cruise ship. The pampering begins with limousine pickup at the airport, an amenity that sets the stage for this refined and curated excursion. After a night in Hanoi, board a lavish Au Co Cruise boat, settle into a plush oversized cabin, and then emerge on deck for a taste of fresh air and exotic scenery. Opportunities to absorb the local culture abound during this adventure. A cooking demonstration by a professional chef offers insight into the vibrant flavors of Vietnamese cuisine, leaving visitors with savory recipes to re-create at home. Depart the ship for a ride on a traditional bamboo boat to the floating village of Vung Vieng, where masterful fishing has sustained generations of locals who have made living in harmony with nature a lifestyle. Revel in the satisfying seclusion of Vung Ha beach, where swimming and kayaking offers access to pristine waters while paddling under archways created by dramatic cliffs. Complete the cruise with a tour of Sung Sot cave, where stalactites cast striking shadows throughout the cavern, creating an overwhelming sensory experience - one that has awed visitors for a thousand years. Senses (and memory card) filled with incredible visions, disembark at Hanoi for free time to explore. This metropolis reveals an intoxicating mix of French and Vietnamese features, blending into a singular locale where street vendors are as likely to dish out spicy bowls of pho as pour steamy cups of cafe au lait. To really locate the heartbeat of a region, exploration off the beaten path is required. Experience the enchantment of northern Vietnam on a journey that takes guests away from the known, while delivering on the finest of details: luxury lodging, delectable dishes, and bewitching beauty.

What's Included

  • Five night luxury guided tour of Hanoi and Halong Bay in northern Vietnam for two.
  • Two day overnight cruise through Halong Bay, Bai Tu Long Bay & Lan Ha Bay.
  • Two night stay aboard the lavish Au Co Cruise Ship in an en-suite cabin with a balcony offering panoramic views.
  • Two nights accommodation in a deluxe room featuring full length bay windows at the chic Melia Hanio Hotel in Hanoi.
  • Biking or walking tour of Cat Ba Island and kayaking or swimming on Vung Ha's secluded beach.
  • Onboard cooking demonstration and Vietnamese tea ceremony offer a true taste of the culture.
  • Guided tours of the Sung Sot cave & the floating fishing village of Vung Vieng by traditional bamboo boat.
  • Two breakfasts, one brunch, two lunches & two dinners, plus water, tea & coffee during the cruise.
  • Roundtrip airport transfers in a Luxtrans Limousine; entrance fees & sightseeing fees
